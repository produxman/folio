The Build

Problem

Legacy billing system couldn't handle complex subscription changes, causing hundreds of thousands of users to experience failed payments and billing confusion. Meister AI's launch required sophisticated usage-based billing that the current system would block.

We built a custom billing engine to eliminate billing friction, reduce churn by ~25%, and enable AI product monetization. The scope covered zero-downtime migration, usage-based billing infrastructure, and self-service subscription management.

Key Goals: Support usage-based AI billing Reduce support tickets ~70% Enable seamless subscription changes
